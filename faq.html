Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is included in an expedition trip?
Each trip includes route planning, local transportation, group camping gear, experienced guides, and optional add-ons.
2. What skill level is required?
We offer beginner, intermediate, and advanced trips. Check each listing or contact us for recommendations.
3. Do you provide gear?
We provide shared equipment (tents, stoves, filters). Bring your own sleeping bag, pack, and clothes.
4. How do I prepare physically?
Train with loaded hikes, elevation gain, and cardio. We’ll send you a fitness prep checklist.
5. What’s your refund policy?
Cancel 30+ days prior for a full refund. 15–29 days gets 50%. Less than 14 days is non-refundable.
6. Can I bring my kids?
We offer family-friendly options! Minimum age and experience vary by trip.
7. Are pets allowed?
Unfortunately no, due to wildlife safety and park regulations.
8. What if the weather is bad?
We run rain or shine unless there’s a dangerous weather alert (lightning, wildfire, floods).
9. How large are the groups?
Trips range from 4–10 participants for a personalized and safe experience.
10. What should I pack?
We provide a gear list and recommendations for each specific trip via email after booking.
11. What if I have medical conditions?
Please disclose all medical concerns beforehand. We’ll help determine trip suitability.
12. Are meals provided?
Most trips include group-prepared breakfasts and dinners. Bring your own snacks/lunches.
13. Can I join solo?
Absolutely! Many adventurers come solo and leave with lifelong friends.
14. Where do we sleep?
We camp in designated wilderness areas or official campgrounds. All sites are vetted.
15. How do you ensure safety?
Guides are trained in wilderness first aid, satellite communication, and local emergency protocols.
16. Can I get cell service?
Most backcountry areas have no signal. Guides carry emergency satellite communicators.
17. Do you offer private/custom trips?
Yes! We can design a custom route and experience tailored for your group’s needs and timeframe.
18. Are there discounts?
Returning guests, students, and early bookers get discounts. Ask about group rates too!
19. What about wildlife?
We teach Leave No Trace, bear safety, and food storage. Wildlife sightings are part of the experience.
20. Can I get more info?
Visit our contact page or calendar to view upcoming trips and send questions.
