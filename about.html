About Adrian

Hi, I’m Adrian Hanna — a full-stack web developer, expedition leader, and lifelong outdoor enthusiast based in Ventura County, California.

This project is a showcase of my technical skills and my passion for backcountry exploration. Whether I’m building modular front-end websites or leading friends up the switchbacks of Mount Whitney, I bring the same level of focus, curiosity, and drive.

Through this site, I combine my experience in digital design, interactive mapping, and trip planning to tell immersive stories. My goal is to inspire others to explore, connect, and create — online and outdoors.

Technical Skills

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
  • Responsive design and accessibility
  • EXIF metadata & lightbox integration
  • SVG animation and custom artwork
  • GPX trail data + interactive maps (planned)

Outdoor Background

I’ve led multi-day treks through the Eastern Sierra, the Grand Staircase–Escalante, and remote backcountry sites across the American Southwest. From snow camping to spring desert crossings, every trip has deepened my love for the wild and my respect for its challenges.

As a solo traveler and group guide, I specialize in helping others reconnect with nature and with each other through memorable experiences, storytelling, and shared hardship.
