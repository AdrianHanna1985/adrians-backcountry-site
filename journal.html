First Sunrise at Cottonwood

August 21, 2021

Woke up to the sharp chill of dawn and brewed instant coffee under the sky’s warmest colors. I hiked out just before 6 AM. No one else around—just me and the scent of juniper in the wind. Today reminded me why I started this project in the first place.

Smoke Above the Alabama Hills

August 23, 2021

Visibility was low from the wildfires north of here, but the haze created a surreal golden glow across the Alabama Hills. I found a few photo gems I didn’t expect. Moments like these make the detours worth it.
